  1. Roadster Sport is a pleasant and refreshing scent for men. This edt is classified as a leather/ tobacco fragrance, though it's non-offensive like certain fragrances in that category. One of the most pronounced notes can be found in the opening, which is the orange mandarin. I think this note in particular gives it that crisp, refreshing, slighty fruity vibe. It maintains the spicy aroma that made the original edt appealing, yet it isn't as strong, which actually works well. It dries down to a soft an subtle spicy/woodsy base, that is noticably pleasant. Projection and longevity are both questionable, as they appear a bit weak on my skin. The original Roadster is certainly stronger in those areas. So for that reason I can only give this one 4 stars, instead of 5.
