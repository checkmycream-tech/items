Should work well in the South Carolina heat and humidity - I can hardly wait..!! It smells great, but lacks the projection/sillage and longevity to be a major player. For the serious collector, or Ralph Lauren fan, add to your collection. If you are a novice in the game, pass on BP#1 - there are plenty of citrus based fragrances out there with better performance.
