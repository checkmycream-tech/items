  2. This perfume confuses me so much. I really want to like it. Looking at the notes, I should be crazy about it. Yet every time I try it on, I am tempted to scrub it off. I get a strongly unpleasant alcohol-like blast when I spritz it on. After a few minutes that is gone but what replaces it is not much better. To describe it I would say if you lit a big pile of pencil shavings on fire, and then threw in a couple of cloves and a couple of wilted gardenia petals, the smoke from that fire would be what this scent smells like on me. That is all I smell until after a couple of hours, at which point I can smell nothing unless I bring my wrist to my nose. On my wrist I smell a powdery, perfume-that's-so-old-its-turned-to-alcohol smell. Sadness. I normally love anything rose! Why can't this perfume be nice to me? I'm not giving up this easily, though. I think I'm going to try layering it with other scents.
