This product is not new to the market, but I've never considered Milani mascaras before until I saw great reviews online for this one. I picked it up in Kmart for around $4 and I think that despite having a cheap price, this mascara really packs a punch. My lashes are straight with medium length, but very fine and blonde and can barely be seen without makeup. A mascara that makes me happy is one that volumizes and defines my lashes, and length is always a nice bonus. Runway Lashes does give pretty decent volume and length. While my lashes don't look like falsies, this mascara does leave a big impact and gives a more dramatic look rather than an everyday look. The color is a very nice, rich, matte black. I didn't have any major clumping, but I could tell that I do have to apply this carefully otherwise my lashes will stick together. Also, I would only stick to two coats at most because any more would also probably cause clumping. My skin and eyelids are oily, but I'm so impressed that I didn't experience any smudging (or flaking), even though it is now very hot and humid here in the southern US. Even with it's all day wear I was able to remove it easily with an oil-free makeup remover. So, final verdict. Pretty decent volume and length, minimal clumping, no smudging and flaking, plus free from parabens, all for $4?? Yes, it's possible with Runway Lashes from Milani. While I wouldn't call this an HG product for me, I must say I'm impressed with how well this product performed. I'd definitely say give this a try if you're looking to change up your mascara routine.
