  1. This set was a great price...so since I was buying online, I took a risk that the vanilla scent would be something I would like. The Island Vanilla scent is just okay. Not the best vanilla scent I've smelled, but fairly pleasant. The roll-on perfume is nice and convenient and I do like the Lip Quench (like a large lip balm, slightly vanilla-scented but with a very subtle almost pearlized look). The only part of it that I didn't like was the lotion. After a couple of days use, the lotion actually gave me a rash where I had put it on my arms, so I quit using it. I do have sensitive skin, but no other scented lotions have ever given me a rash. And I've tried Pacifica's French Lilac set and have had no problems with the lotion in that one...and the scent is a light and pretty floral I like quite a bit. So, sorry to say, Island Vanilla only gets 3 stars from me, for just okay.
