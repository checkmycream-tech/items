One was nice and stiff (befitting such a heavy blade) while the other was quite a bit looser. A few taps on the pivot pin with a ball peen hammer on the anvil of my bench vise fixed this without issue. There is no play between the blade and scales or between the scales themselves in relation to one another (i.e., a decently solid build). Etching on the scales is deeper on one knife than on the other, and blade grinds are a bit vague and wavy, though not terrible. Again, for basically $10 shipped, these are a bargain, and about the level of fit and finish I expected.
