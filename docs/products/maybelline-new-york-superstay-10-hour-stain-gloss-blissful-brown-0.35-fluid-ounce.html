I think people aren't sure how to use this product and therefore don't get the most out of it. It was recommended by an Ulta rep to me so I gave it a go. What you want to do: don't treat it like a traditional stain where you want one very thin coat. Put on 1 coat, then layer on another. Let it dry a loooong time. I would give it a good 5 minutes and put on your other makeup. It is also advisable to use a primer under it (I just use the normal primer for your face and put some on my lips). After letting it fully dry I use a little Philosophy lip gloss over it. I apply it very carefully...barely touching my lips. But this gives my lips moisture so the color isn't drying and I have had a lot of luck with this product lasting all day when I follow this process. It also fades nicely and evenly, no areas of color randomly missing. I actually LOVE this product and the colors are vibrant and beautiful. Give it a go!
