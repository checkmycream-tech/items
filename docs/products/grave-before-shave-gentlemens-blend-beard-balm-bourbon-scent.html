  17. I make men's products (balms, oils, soaps) as a hobby. I really enjoy trying as many products as I can to see what others are doing. Grave Before Shave/Fisticuffs is probably my favorite brand going. They have many things going for them and I don't really have anything negative to point out.   18. Love this beard balm! Contrary to what another reviewer (jokingly I think) writes, it does not smell like alcohol, nor will you smell like an alcoholic using it. The scent is more like others have described it being more of a manly cologne scent with hints of sandalwood. Just manly, musky, and cool. The scent also subtlety sticks with you throughout the day, especially if you are in the habit of contemplatively stroking your beard every once in while like all cool bearded gentleman do. And although I like the scent, what I love most about this balm is how it works! It's fabulous. It goes on smooth and is quickly soaked up by my beard. Other balms I have tried leave my hands and beard greasy; not this one. It makes my beard soft and smooth. I also like the hold it gives as it tames and helps me to style my beard the way I wish, especially my mustache. I also have to say I am impressed with the company: from the branding, the products I have tried so far (Original and Gentleman's beard balm), their customer service, their employees, and the overall edgy, cool vibe that Fisticuffs/Grave Before Shave has, I'm a huge fan!
