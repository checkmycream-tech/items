He didn't pick up as much of the floral notes as I did and he thought it smelled lemony and clean. It has NO DIMETHICONE or silicone (which, according to some, are bad for hair and defeating the purpose of healthy hair if used regularly) like many serums for shine and moisture. In the month or two that I used this serum the condition of my normally frizzy, baby-fine, damaged, and dry hair improved. The improvement was not temporary or superficial (as is the result with most hair care products). By doing what it claims (and what I believe it did) and penetrating the hair cortex, it moisturized my hair without weighing it down, banished fly-aways, and prevented split ends and breakage. Now that I have been using thicker oils instead, I realize what a good product this serum is. It makes all the difference in the moisture of the hair- minus the overgreased factor. Just apply it to the ends if you are concerned about getting too greased up. *UPDATE* *Be careful with the bottle. Mine broke when I dropped it on my bathroom floor. It is very thin glass. (I've never broken any other bottles in my bathroom- ever.)* Also, I realized that during the time when I was using this serum my hair did GROW much faster- or more correctly I RETAINED my hair growth (you don't use it on the scalp, it's not rogaine- it's for moisture). My hair was thicker and I was having to get frequent trims (every 3 weeks) to keep my hair at shoulder length. This is a product that is very different from thicker oils. There is no comparison. This product is DEFINITELY WORTH A TRY if you are trying to grow your hair and have damage/dryness (or if you just have damage/dryness). ~ Now that I want longer hair (and highlights have recked my hair so that I won't be retaining much growth) I am putting away my other oils and going to get another bottle of this stuff.~ 1/2/12 Yet another update: I do not recommend using this product more than once a day. It will make your hair greasy, although your hair will absorb the excess in about a day. I recommend using it only at night, only on the ends of your hair. You won't feel like you smell like lemons/grapefruit when you leave the house, and you won't overdo it and get flat, greasy hair. Also: this is not a magic bullet for split ends. When my hair was much healthier (pre-highlights) I felt like this worked better to prevent split ends. I am still getting a lot of split ends, despite this product, because split ends are just what happen when your hair is unhealthy. Pre-highlights I did keratin smoothing treatments (every 2-3 months) and traditional relaxers (a few times a year). This really helped then. Regarding retaining growth: My hair is growing but I am still having trouble retaining the length. I have 2 inch \\"roots\\" showing but my hair is not a full 2\\" longer. I have been supplementing with another oil, therefore not using this as consistently. When I used this before (and noticed much better results), this was just about the only product I used on my hair besides shampoo and conditioner. In other words- CONSISTENCY HELPS. If you really want this to workits best, use it regularly. THE POINT: This will really help if your hair is dry, minimally chemically treated, and in decent to good condition. If your hair is really damaged- this product may not save you- but it's likely that nothing but time and trims will.
