  8. Great for your teeth but not minty enough
  9. I used this toothpaste for about 1.5 years without a problem, but last fall started developing cracking/dryness in the corner of my lips. At first I wasn't sure what was causing it, but finally figured out it was the toothpaste. I stopped using it for about 6 weeks and had no problems, and a few weeks ago thought I'd try again (using a new tube). Within days, the cracking & dryness came back. I don't know if they changed their formula or whether there are ingredients in the toothpaste that some people just can't tolerate. (Others have reported similar problems for other Arm & Hammer toothpastes in Amazon reviews, including sores under the tongue and swelling in the mouth for a whitening toothpaste). If you develop these problems, discontinue the toothpaste. Arm & Hammer needs to warn people about these potential problems on their product which is why they're getting only 2 stars. I did like the toothpaste before I had these problems. (For the first few times I brushed my teeth, the taste of baking soda seemed awful but I stuck with it and eventually came to like the flavor quite a bit - it leaves a fresh taste in the mouth. I also haven't had any cavities since starting using the toothpaste; I can't absolutely attribute this to the toothpaste but perhaps it does a better job of preventing cavities than other toothpastes. Unfortunately, it seems to be irritating my mouth now.)
