For the record, I've tried a full facial application several times, stunningly no breakouts, just prefer to to keep sunscreen in the mix. Other Shea butter products tend to cause pimples for me. Absorbs fast and leaves skin soft and dry yet moisturized. The product smells very ~natural~. Many products seem to claim exoteric ingredients and only include them to the extent required to market them. This product has a significant bee smell, though I find it absorbs or disasters quickly as well. I definitely recommend this product, it has convinced me bee-products are extremely healthy for skin and hair.
