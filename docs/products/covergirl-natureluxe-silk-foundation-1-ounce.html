  5. I have always been scared of liquid foundation. I have oily skin, and even though I follow a great regimen, liquids always seemed too dense and difficult. When I got the sample of the NatureLuxe foundation, I got online and found as many tutorials as I could. I used a brush instead of a sponge, I buffed the color out into my hairline, and I let it set. It was super easy. I was actually surprised at how good it felt on my face, and how fast it was to put on. Unfortunately, I chose a color that ended up being a little dark, so I will have to go shopping for another color, but I think I liked it enough to purchase it again. The foundation felt soft and did a great job evening out my skin tone (except for the fact that I looked a little bronzed). By the end of the day, I felt like the foundation and my oily skin were starting to clash. As a first-timer, I didn't think about things like blotting, or re-powdering throughout the day. Next time, I will do those things and see what happens. I definitely felt confident about my skin, which is the best thing a make-up can do. I docked the foundation a star because of the end-of-the-day extreme need to wash my face, and also because the package is very small.
