I recently stumbled upon a BP mineral makeup starter kit for less than $10 (yay!) at one of my favorite discount stores and thought I'd give it a try. I have been very pleasantly surprised at the quality of the makeup and the kabuki brush that came with the kit. The BP brush does an excellent job of picking up, storing and applying the mineral makeup. I'm not sure if it's the makeup or the brush, but I seem to achieve a softer, smoother, lighter and more even coverage than I had with the bare minerals. Upon comparing the BP brush to my bare minerals brush, the BP brush is MUCH softer to the touch. I can't help but think it must, therefore, be much easier on my skin, as well. I plan to gradually replace my existing brush collection with BP brushes. I anticipate this brush would compare favorably to any competitor's product and recommend it without reservation. Mine was free as part of a kit, but, I would not hesitate to purchase it (or other BP brushes) if needed, particularly at this price, which is a bargain compared to the price on the BP web site. I hope this review helps. All the best!
