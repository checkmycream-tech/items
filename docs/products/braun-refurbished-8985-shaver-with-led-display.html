The wiskers do not build up inside the cutting head like on lots of shavers. Most of them fall outside of the shaver and into the sink. Since I am not a daily shaver, sometimes not even weekly, the Braun gets a bit of a workout one in a while when my beard gets to 1/4 to 3/8\\" long. It tends to pull hairs out rather than shave them when they are that long. So what I do to get around it is to use the side burn trimmer to cut all of the hair down short then use the shaver normally and it does a great job. Just a couple of extra minutes to cut facial hairs down short enough to be painlessly shaved. I do have some stubborn neck hairs that I have to use the side burn trimmer to get. I like this shaver!
