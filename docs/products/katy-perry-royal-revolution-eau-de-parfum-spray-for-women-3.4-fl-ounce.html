  11. I am a huge fan of Katy Perry and was excited to see that she would have new fragrances come out. Unfortunately, I wasn't a big fan of the original Killer Queen fragrance but was happy to see she would come out with different versions of the original. I finally managed to get my hands on the scent and it smells so much better than Killer Queen. Like most reviews say it's a little bit lighter in smell and more floral/fruity, which I love. Not only that but the bottle and packaging it came in is extremely adorable and the color of the bottle is very pretty deep blue. However, the only thing that will probably prevent me from replenishing this scent in the future is that it does not last. Typically, most perfumes, I only spray it a couple times and it lasts for half the day. With the Royal Revolution, though, I spray about 10x as much as that and within an hour or so I cannot smell it. I even spray some on my wrists (which is my most sensitive area) and not even then could I smell something. I will probably keep the bottle once I am done with this fragrance but I would not recommend spending the amount of money on a perfume that needs to be reapplied every hour. Wish I could like it more!
