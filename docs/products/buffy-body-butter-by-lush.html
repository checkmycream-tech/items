I have sensitive skin. For me, cutting the bar into small pieces doesn't work as well as just keeping the bar dry and storing it outside the shower. To use Buffy, I get myself clean in the shower first then either step out of the tub or turn the water off temporarily. I use a washcloth in my hand to help me keep a good grip on the bar, and use light, circular motions to buff my skin. I usually just cover my legs, backside, and upper arms, then hop back into the shower for a quick rinse, and I do use an additional moisturizer after the shower, since I don't cover my whole body with Buffy. I use the bar about once a week and never on my legs if I've just shaved them. The great thing about Buffy for people with sensitive skin is that the ingredients used are super fresh and natural. Overall, like many others out there, I highly recommend Buffy, even for people with sensitive skin.
