  4. I'm not that crazy about Organix Moroccan Argan Oil Conditioner but that being said out of the daily conditioners I have used by Organix's product lines this is the best one I have tried. I have used their Tea Tree, Grapefruit Mango Butter and Rejuvenation Cherry Blossom Genseng as well and never cared for any of them. This formula, the Organix Moroccan Argan Oil Conditioner isn't the best daily conditioner for the most part but it has grown on me. The reason is the conditioner is a lot like their other brands. It isn't thick and while in my hair it doesn't have that slippery feel to it as some conditioners do but does detangle my hair and when I get out of the shower my hair remains tangle free and even soft. I've written reviews on other conditioners and have read up on ingredients of shampoo and conditioners. Several conditioners I have tried in the past has silicon in it , this provides the slippery feel to your hair while in the shower but as soon as you step out of the shower the same silicon will cause your hair to tangle worse. I use another conditioner which has Moroccan Argan Oil in it and it has both the silicon and the Argan oil . I originally thought I preferred the combination as it not only provided the slippery feel in my hair while showering but once out of the shower the Argan oil prevented my hair from re-tangling so no additional leave in conditioner was needed. However after a while this formula (silicon and oil) builds up and leaves my hair feeling dirty and weighed down. After a month of using the Organix Moroccan Argan Oil Conditioner I haven't noticed this side effect and my hair feels clean even after multiple uses. I also appreciate that Organix Moroccan Argan Oil Conditioner doesn't smell nutty or extremely sweet. Rather is smells clean, almost like Ivory soap with a slightly sweet undertone. The scent isn't over powering but I can smell the scent in my hair when I hold my hair up close or my husband can and he likes it. Every conditioner / Shampoo I have used which had Moroccan Argan Oil or most other type of oils , always has a nut or over powering sweet smell to it. I don't mind those scents at first but I quickly tire of it.
