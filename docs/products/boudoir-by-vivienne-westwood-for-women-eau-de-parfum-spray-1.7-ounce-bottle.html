  9. Boudoir has been my signature scent for years. I get plenty of compliments from men and women alike. It's an exciting, sensual, powdery, scent with floral undertones. There is the original version which has a dark pink (almost brownish/orange at times), and then a later version which is light pink appearing more pastel in color. The difference in smell and wearability is night/day in my opinion. The original is a sensual, lingering, pleasant, longer-lasting scent. Whereas, the later version is powdery, short-lived, with undertones of grasses/herbs rather than florals which sent my allergies into a talespin! No matter which version, Boudoir is definitely not for everyone. You either love it or you hate it - there is no in between. Before you decide to get hooked on it - remember, the perfume has been discontinued and is VERY hard to find, and price will vary drastically.
