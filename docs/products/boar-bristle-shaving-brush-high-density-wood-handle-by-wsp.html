The glue bulges into the brush, prohibiting the hair from moving like you would expect it to. Also, the hair seems to be significantly taller in the center - disproportionate to the hairs on the edges. Whether that's a result of the gluing, I'm not sure of, but the result is an odd brush to use. On the flipside, the handle is well coated and the brush certainly lathers... and well. It's just not the most comfortable, attractive or easiest to use. Even at the low price point, there are better options - hopefully newer versions will be improved.
