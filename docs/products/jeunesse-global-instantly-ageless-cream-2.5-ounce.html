Effect lasts about 6 hours in my experience, if you don't wash you face or visit the sauna. IMPORTANT this is NOT an anti-wrinkle serum or cream that may (or may not) decrease the severity of wrinkles on the face OVER TIME. Instantly Ageless is what I use for a quick fix when I am going out to a function or just want to look a little bit better. It does the job perfectly and I am satisfied with it. Being over 60 and male (I don't wear make-up . . . and no I am not vain, just like to look as young as I feel . . . lol) is I suppose in the age bracket that would see or show the most benefit (45 - 75).
