I wear this, paired with some mascara and neutral eye shadow and it suffices as my everyday makeup.<br /><br />This is the first primer I have ever used, and I like it. The only reason I am searching for a new one is because I would like to find one that is naturally derived and yet functional. Otherwise, I wholeheartedly recommend it.
