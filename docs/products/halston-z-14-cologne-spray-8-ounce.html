If you want a cheap way to smell like cinnamon, here is a great option.<br /><br />All the rave reviews here are probably based on vintage stock of this fragrance before the oak moss was removed and the formula was changed to Eau de Cinnamon. I'm not sure why anyone would want this these days, at least in the modern formulation. If you want a cheap good male fragrance, Azzarro Pour Homme, Grey Flannel by G. Z14 is not unpleasant, and is certainly the best cheap fragrance you can find at the average Target. (Though even there Stetson and Old Spice are highly competitive...) Just be forewarned that if you get the modern reformulation, you are getting exactly what you pay for: a simple, linear, short-lived fragrance.
