Some reviewers commented that with longer stubble it yanked hairs, but I haven't it do that any more than any other electric shaver -- I regularly shave off several days of hair and I just move it a bit slower when doing that. A full charge lasts about ten shaves for me (almost an hour of runtime?). Blade speed seems to slow a bit if you let the battery get close to empty.
