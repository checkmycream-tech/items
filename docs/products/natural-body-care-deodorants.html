I do notice that if I go to the gym and work out hard or go running I will smell. However at work, I have no issues with the longevity of the deodorant. If I will be especially sweaty, I will reapply it and there are no issues. Of note, this is not an antiperspirant and you will still sweat!<br /><br />2) The foaming action... when I first put this on in the morning after showering, it does foam up. I believe this may be intentional as this product has cotton extract in it to theoretically wick away sweat (however I don't know for sure). I don't know how efficacious this necessarily is, but the foam goes away after I dry out and does not bother me in the least.<br /><br />3) The stick of deodorant lasts a long time for me. I usually go through deodorant relatively quickly and this seems to last a bit longer (a couple months) compared to other brands I had previously used.<br /><br />Previous to this, I had been using Tom's of Maine Unscented and developed a terrible rash. I tried Trader Joes Unscented and I would smell pretty gross within an hour (similar to how the solid crystal types of deoderants worked for me). I also tried Naturally Fresh Liquid Roller and it also gave me a pretty bad rash (although it did keep me smelling fresh). For me, this one worked. I do know people's body chemistries are different, so try different kinds and see what works for you. This one happened to work great for me!<br /><br />UPDATE:<br />So I hate to do this, but I'm taking away a star. After using this deodorant for a while, its starting to not work as well for me (I don't know if its just variation in the bars or what). Its not really bad, but there are definitely some days where I smell really really gross. Just something to keep in mind. I can usually reapply, but not always. Otherwise, it definitely doesn't irritate my underarms which is important to me - but if it gets any worse in strength of scent, I might have to try some others which might last a little longer throughout the day.
