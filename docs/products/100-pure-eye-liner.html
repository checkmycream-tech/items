  7. I was so excited to see all natural products, as I have contact dermatitis to many chemicals. I ordered mascara, lip gloss, eye shadow and eye liner. Loved the creamstick eye liner. Unfortunately, within an hour my eyes were watering, bright red, itching and throbbing. This lasted almost 24 hours. I did some research and found that tocopheral-a is natural vitamin E with acetic acid added to it aka carboxylic acid, which I am allergic to. There are many articles on the dermatis, blisters and problems with tocopheral-a. I wrote to the manufacturer, Purity Cosmetics, asking for the MSDS/label, materials safety data sheet. It's been over three days and I have received no response to them. Update: Purity Cosmetics uses tocopheral alpha and not tocopheral acetate. The company checked the lot numbers on my make-up and found that this was old make-up. They replaced all make-up and I have had no issues. This is a wonderful eye liner and with no reaction and fresh product, I'm changing to 5 stars, especially after the company was so helpful.
