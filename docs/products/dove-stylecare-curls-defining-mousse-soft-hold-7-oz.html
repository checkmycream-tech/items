Then I let it dry by itself. As long as I get my hair saturated while it's still wet (not soaking wet, but before it gets just \\"damp\\") then it has beautiful shine and definition. It is a little oily, but my hair looks shiny and healthy, and the curls are beautiful. The smell is not the same as the rest of the Dove products, but it doesn't bother me, and I am sensitive to strong smells. I was previously using DevaCurl (shampoo, conditioner, and gel), but it was making my head break out. I haven't had a breakout problem since switching to Dove. All in all, I could see where this product would not work for quite a few people, but it's great for me.
