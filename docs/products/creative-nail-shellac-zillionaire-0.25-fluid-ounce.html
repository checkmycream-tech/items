  1. I had my nails done with Zillionaire on March 2. Five days later they still look freshly painted. This color is basically clear with multi color flecks of glitter. I have on 2 coats and it looks great. I was curious about the Shellac manicure since my spa just started offering it. Initially I chose Romantique but it looked streaky and was not opaque. It was removed using the acetone pads from CND, and took about 10 minutes to soak. The nail tech used a stick looking tool to gently scrape the polish from my nail, it begins to crystallize into a gummy powder. My spa charges $44 for the Shellac manicure, which in my opinion is ridiculous. I think I'm going to invest in my own UV light and polish and just do it myself. Overall Zillionaire is a great color choice if you want something that isn't flashy, but still interesting!
  2. This is a great clear polish with larger irradescent glitter than typical glitter polishes.
