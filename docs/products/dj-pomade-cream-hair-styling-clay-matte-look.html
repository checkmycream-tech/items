He has tried a couple different hair gels, one that we paid quite a bit for from the salon but they did not style his hair like he wanted. They also left his hair greasy looking and feeling and he was less than impressed.<br /><br />This is a huge amount of cream compared to the others we bought. It is twice the size of the one we got at the salon and we paid more for the salon product. The first thing you notice when you take the lid off is the fabulous smell. The best way I can describe it is somewhat like musk. The product is not greasy at all. I actually rubbed some into my hands and it was totally absorbed.<br /><br />My husband was impressed at how little it took to style his hair. He started out with product the size of a pea to do his initial spiking and used about half that amount afterwards to fine tune his look. His hair looked like there wasn't even product in it. It was not shiny at all and his hair held the spike all night which included a lot of dancing. I believe we have found his new hair cream!
