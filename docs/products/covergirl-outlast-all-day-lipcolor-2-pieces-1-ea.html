  23. I have used this lipstick every day for just over a week now, so that I could give some informed feedback. I am giving this 4 stars, not because there's anything wrong with the lipstick, but really because I'm lazy. This stuff works great for what it is, but I don't always remember to re-apply the balm that goes on top. Now, the color will stay in place, and feel incredibly natural and comfortable IF you remember to re-apply the balm. If you forget, and leave it too long, the lipstick dries out and starts rapidly flaking and peeling off your lips. If you can keep up with it, believe me, it will stay as advertised. It will stay after you shower, after sleeping and even after eating. It only barely started to come off in the inner area of my lips, after eating greasy fried chicken. However, I would like to mention one small caveat, that is sugary foods like glazed donuts or ice cream will remove the balm, and cause the lipstick to start coming off pretty rapidly. My advice, if you know you're going to eat something sugary, wear another lipstick, or bring some makeup remover so you can remove and re-apply it after you eat. This is a great longwear lipstick, and it definitely will stay on, if you can keep up with it better than I do. If you can't you should expect it to peel and flake.
