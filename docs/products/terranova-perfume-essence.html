Determined, I went in search of this perfume in my local shops around the Pacific Northwest city I live in, but alas I was unsuccessful and gave up. Fast forward 10 years and I happened to catch a similar scent on the air as I walked through a local boutique. I inquired if they sold the perfume and was told they didn't, but this time I was undeterred. So I went home to log on to my friend Amazon.com and found Terranova Pikake Perfume Essence. I carefully read the reviews, comparing options and took the leap of faith that the Terranova brand would bring me back to the scent from years ago. It arrived practically the next day and much to the delight of my olfactory sense, the Terranova Pikake Perfume Essence was exactly what I had hoped for! It is truly the essence of Pikake and smells heavenly. The bottle is not large, but believe me you don't need to use much. In fact, I bought a small spritz bottle (a travel sized one) and poured half of the Pikake oil into it so that I could use it as a spray. I have 1/4 of that spritz bottle left, plus the remaining half in the original Terranova bottle after 4 months in my daily rotation of perfume. The icing on this cake is that my husband is wild about the scent on me so it's a win-win-win all the way around!
