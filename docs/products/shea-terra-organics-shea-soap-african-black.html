In fact I had been testing cream eye shadows on the back of my hand and decided to use this soap to try to wash it off. The first lather barely budged the eye shadow (something to be said for the eye shadow there!) I rinsed and my hand was still covered in eye shadow and felt really filmy from the soap. I washed again and rinsed immediately and everything came off- everything! I was impressed :) I have been using this for almost 2 months now and just in the last week or so my face seems to be clearing up. I am now 6 months pregnant, so it could just be the hormones, but I will continue to use the black soap and see how things develop.<br /><br />UPDATE<br />8/11/13: I decided to ditch this soap. I don't really have anything bad to say about it, but nothing great either. My first impression rating of 3 stars was pretty accurate. My skin cleared up, but I'm pretty sure it had nothing to do with this product, it was hormonal acne :/ Now that I have a newborn I really just don't have time for leaving it on for 5 minutes and washing again. It was kind of a nice ceremonious thing back when I just had my toddler, but now I can't be bothered.<br />Bottom line: it doesn't work for me, but I don't think it's a bad product.E<br />8/11/13: I decided to ditch this soap.
