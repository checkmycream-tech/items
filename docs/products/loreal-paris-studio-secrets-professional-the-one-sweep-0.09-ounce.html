The first time I used it, I did not use a primer on my lids. Now, I have oily skin and a primer is almost always necessary for me. Within an hour, the eyeshadow had faded and started to smudge. This may be because I had no primer. I have yet to try it with a primer though so I will do that before completely disregarding the product. 2. The color distinction is a bit overwhelming...I have large eyes but really, my lids are not that large, so the deep purple \\"liner\\" color at the bottom looked like it was too think in comparison to the read of the color. Usually I like a thinner liner in the corners of my eyes, thickening the line as I move to the outer counters for a more dramatic effect. 3. This did not work in one single sweep. It always takes a few more sweeps to apply the color completely, which kind of defeats its purpose... All in all, I gave it three stars. I like the coors themselves and I think they do make my brown eyes really pop. I liked the idea of having a quick eyeshadow that is easy to apply and helps to eliminate the time consuming process of priming, contouring, highlighting, and lining...But I think I like the results better doing it the old fashioned way. Will I use the shadow again? Definitely. Will I purchase another set? Perhaps but probably doubtful. I can get the same effect with the other shadows I currently own. This eyeshadow will come in handy when I just want something kind of quick...and like I said, I like the colors...but I think even when I'm in a hurry, I will feel better if I use a blending brush to smooth out the lines and of course, I'll be sure to add some primer first as well. One last \\"pro\\": I did like the size of the compact and I like that it has a mirror built in. Good for touch ups on the go and it will fit neatly inside a small purse. I love L'oreal so this one product will not keep me from buying other products from the brand. (See my review for the Illuminating mascara as well.)
