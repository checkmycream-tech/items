  1. This is a great styling product if your hair is thin/fine and curly. Usually I combine this product with just a dime-sized amount of Ouidad's Moisture Lock Gel and either blowdry or let it air dry. On days that I want to add even more volume, I add PlayCurl Volumizing Styling Gel Spray first, then the foam and blowdry my hair with a diffuser. I've found that I use less of all the ouidad products (maybe because my hair is just above my shoulders or it's fine, not sure which). Love the fact that my hair doesn't feel like it has product in it at all and yet holds it's style for most of the day (depends on the weather here). Since I've started using these products, I've gotten plenty of compliments on my curls. Finally, I like my curls. You won't find me trying to hide my curls by pulling my hair back into a ponytail anymore. I've also used Schwarzkopf Bonacure Moisture Kick line of products (which works as well for me) however,I do not like the chemical smell that Schwarzkopf products seem to have. Ouidad products smell so much better.

I have tried a couple other volumizing foams, but they left my hair dry and did nothing to enhance the natural curl, not to mention I never noticed more volume after using them. But Ouidad's Volumizing Foam has delivered its volume claim. I can see a noticeable difference, especially at the roots of my hair. It does not provide quite the curl definition that using a gel does, but personally, I think I like the soft, slightly looser curls this product give me better. I do not wash my hair every day, and on day 2 of a wash when using this product, I am able to dampen my hair with Ouidad's Refreshing Spray, re-scrunch (without adding more product), let it dry naturally and have nice curls again. I would recommend experimenting with the amount of product used to find what works best for you because it really depends on the thickness of your hair. Ouidad also recommends using their &#34;rake and shake&#34; technique with this product, but I find just scrunching it into my hair works better for me. In reading other reviews, I noticed several comments about the strong scent of this product.
