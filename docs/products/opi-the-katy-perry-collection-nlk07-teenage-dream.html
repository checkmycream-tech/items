A word on removal: glitter polishes can be frustrating to remove the traditional way (and scrubbing your nails isn't good, as it will make the glitter scratch them). A handy method is the tinfoil method, which is as follows: soak 10 cotton balls in acetone remover as you usually would, but instead of wiping your nail, put one soaked cotton ball on each nail and wrap a strip of tinfoil around each one to hold it in place. This allows the remover to soak through all the layers of glitter. Leave for 5 to 10 minutes, then apply pressure as you remove the foil and cotton ball. This should remove most if not all of the glitter, and it will be easy to wipe off the bits that are left. It does work--trust me, if I hadn't discovered this trick, I would have forever sworn off glitter polishes because of the removal issues.
