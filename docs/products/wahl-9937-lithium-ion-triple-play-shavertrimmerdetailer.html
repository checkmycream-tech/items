Fool proof. It blasted my 3 month winter beard down in less then a minute. NEVER saw a trimmer/shaver blast through my thick winter beard like this did. It's a glutton for punishment I swear. Didn't bog down either. Just kept hammering through it. When it was a good bit better then stubble length (I purposely put it to the torture test by leaving my beard a bit longer then I should have) I slide the trimmer off, and got the rotary heads on. Like the Thunder of the Gods it went to work! Yes it did! Never bogged down, just asked if I could hammer on it some more. UNREAL! Virtually no pulling. VERY SMOOTH! It had been years since I used a rotary so I took a minute to perfect my technique. Once that happened, it roared through my 1/4 inch beard like nobody's business. Glutton for punishment! The shaver produces a bit of noise, but no more then other rotary shavers. It's what you want. Something that simply SOUNDS like it MEANS business. And it does! Hammered my beard! Clean up was a bit tricky. I discovered I had to use the brush to loosen it up, thenI put the top plate back down, gave it a little tap, and most of it fell out. Clean up was pretty easy. Oh, and the top plate is pure genius. In the past i've lost the top plates in various ways. There's no way to loose it. It has a swing up mechanism that keeps it from getting lost even by clumsy fingers......like mine lol! If ur looking a great rotary/trimmer shaver......this is it! Bar none the best shaver i've had since my first Remington in the late 80's.
