I have to say that I purchased this because I am a stay at home Mom of 6 year old twins, and that means that I don't have much time to do my nails, and when I do them, I would like the polish to last more than a day or two... I had previously used the Sally Hanson Sensationail system with good results, but the cost of their replacement base and top coats made me want to search for a cheaper alternative. That being said, I got these in the mail, and with high hopes, I popped in a movie for the kids (my own 2 and the 2 and 7 year old that I also watch during the day) and I got started. Having done this before, I put on some primer, base coat, two coats of polish and the topcoat, ( with a minute under the uv light after each coat) and then I used rubbing alcohol to remove the gummy layer on top. Two nails came out OK, and the other 8 ranged from some mild smearing to the entire shebang coming off when I wiped it... So, I was disappointed to say the least. After the kids went to bed for the night I decided to give it another try... This time, I made sure that I used VERY THIN coats of the base and topcoats (which is tricky because they are quite thick) and then I used the UV light for 2 minutes for each coat. I also noticed that putting on the first coat of polish is difficult, because it doesn't seem to stick to the base coat, but going over it a few times I could lay down a fairly good base and the second coat covers just fine... Anyway, this method did seem to work. The polish dried, my nails are very strong, and so far the polish has been on for about a week with no cracking or lifting. So, in summary, takes a bit longer than some of the other polishes out there, but if you have patience it will work and for the price I think it's worth it.
