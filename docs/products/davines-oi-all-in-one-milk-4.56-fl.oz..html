  72. To answer Erin's question on defective bottles not being covered, diversion and black market products are a multi-billion dollar business. We (I'm a stylist/salon owner), have to sign contracts saying we won't sell to third parties, but only to our clients. This is because it is very easy to manufacture fake products and add fragrance. An example is that recently, the very popular Moroccan Oil was being sold online. When the manufacturer purchased and tested it, it was baby oil and fragrance. Obviously a black market fake. (Do you remember the old Paul Mitchell commercials about real vs. fake?). Because this is a huge problem, and manufacturers can only go after the illegal outfit and not the seller, they have to protect themselves from pirated or fake products however they can. Davines puts millions into making exceptional products, and they want to make sure you are buying the real thing. Hope this sheds some light on a dirty, not so little secret.
