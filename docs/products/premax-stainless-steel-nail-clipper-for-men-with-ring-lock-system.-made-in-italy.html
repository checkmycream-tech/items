I don't know of any other clipper that handles toenails as well, including the plier-type clippers that podiatrists use. To release the handles, push the post to the left or right (it moves with a circular motion). The Premax clippers are very attractively designed, & are lightweight, due to the open architecture of the design. My only concern is how well they'll keep their sharpness. Overall, a great value for the price.
