  1. While I really wanted this product to be the solution to my beauty needs, it's just...not. I'm one of those people that can't remember to freshen their makeup to save their lives, so I was looking for something to help my lipstick (especially) stay put. It does do that...but it drys out your lips so completely and clumps, weirdly enough. Unfortunately, putting a lip balm over top removes the waterproofing aspect and the makeup's back to being...mobile.<br /><br />It's definitely not an item I'd purchase again.
  2. I used this on top of my MAC matte lipstick to stop it from transferring when I eat and drink. It pass the drinking test but didn't pass the eating test. If your food isn't oily, it would pass but if you are eating something oily, the lipstick will still come off. There was no smell or taste to it and it dried matte. It didn't make my lips feel any drier than normal but I used lip primer before the lipstick and Beauty Genius Gel.
  3. It's great to use as a 'setter' of sorts. I was directed to it to use to set lipstick. It's sticky at first, but apply it to whatever (eyebrows, eyeshadow, lipstick) and hold still for a minute. It'll dry and feel normal.

A trip to the mirror revealed my lipstick was actually flaking off due to how it dried. I've yet to use it again for lips, but I did use it on top of my eyeliner--and this worked--except it made my eyeliner look a little dull. Almost matte-ish. Small price to pay I suppose.
