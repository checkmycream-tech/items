Works fine dry though, as long as I have not let my beard grow out for a couple of days.<br /><br />If I let my beard grow out for a few days, it just does not cut the longer hairs reliably. I have to run over them with a separate trimmer first to get them short enough for the shaver to cut them.<br /><br />The screens don't last long - maybe 2 months. They develop holes in them pretty quick. Once that happens you are out of luck. As best I can tell, the screens are only available in a package that includes the cutter blades so I am forced to always replace and pay for both, even though the cutter blades seem fine.<br /><br />For some reason the trimmer on mine no longer extends when I press the extend button. I can pry it out with my fingers, but the trimmer head does not seem to work at all.<br /><br />Based on my experiences with it, I cannot really recommend it, although it does work and under the right conditions (dry and not too long) it does a good job.<br /><br />I only use mine about 6 or 7 months of the year as around Thanksgiving I start growing a beard that I don't shave off until around Easter. I am not sure, but I think this is its 3rd season in use.<br /><br />I noticed some other reviews complained it was noisy. I don't see it as especially noisy, at least it is not so noisy it bothers me any.
