A lot of sunscreens are chemicals that soak in, which your skin might not like. This product uses zinc oxide, which sits on top of the skin. This physical method of blocking the sun's rays has been in use for a long time, but the reason it went out of fashion is because since it won't \\"rub in\\" it left a white residue on the face. Today, they can make the zinc particles a lot finer, which helps, but the product is still meant to form a barrier between your skin and pollutants instead of being absorbed.<br /> Zinc is also used in baby products for diaper rash b/c it's so gentle.
