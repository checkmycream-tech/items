The problem is that when you add water to it, the paint peels off the inside and clogs up the sprayer rendering it unusable. If you decide to buy it anyway and your sprayer suddenly stops working, check to see if the paint has come off inside the bottle. If it has, DO NOT dump the water out in your sink, all that paint can clog your drain. I do not recommend this product.
