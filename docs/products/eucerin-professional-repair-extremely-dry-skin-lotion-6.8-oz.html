What I have found after using this lotion is that it seems to do a good job of moisturizing my hands if I begin applying it regularly at the first sign of my hands becoming extremely dry. If I wait a few hours too long and my hands have already begun to crack, it burns when I put it on. Also, one other small complaint that I have about this lotion is that it seems to feel a little tacky at times. I always apply it on clean hands, but even so, I feel that I need to wash it off and start over again when it begins to feel tacky. This happens quite regularly, probably every 3-4 times I use it. Overall, I think this lotion is worth a try if you are suffering from extremely dry hands like I do. It seemed a little expensive at first, but after receiving the bottle in the mail, I realized that it is a nice size and the lotion goes a long ways.
