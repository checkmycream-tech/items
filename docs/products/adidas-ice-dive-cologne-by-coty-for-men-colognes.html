Despite being an alcohol based cologne, it doesn't smell artificial to me which is impressive. The notes that Amazon has listed are citrus, greens, and amber. That's a bit of a broad list, but I can smell the citrus and amber for sure. Ice Dive is a well put together fragrance without being \\"sporty\\", I would say Ice Dive could be worn whenever for any occasion. The longevity is alright, maybe three, four hours if you spray it appropriately on hot spots like the neck, wrists and dab it behind the ears but I wouldn't give it any more time than that. But with a 3.4 oz bottle for less than ten dollars there's really no need to be stingy.<br /><br />All in all, this would be a great fragrance for spritzing on before work, or going out with friends, or if your lady friend is visiting and you just don't feel like going all out.
