Based on the other good reviews, I bought this razor with some skepticism because I had been shaving with a blade razor for the last 20 years and lately have gotten tired of the rising prices of cartridges and the little knicks I would get using a blade. I have dark, course hairs, so I didn't have high expections for this chargeable razor. My review is based on using the razor DRY, and I cannot comment on using it in the shower. TIP #1: I found that by using the razor after getting out of the shower and toweling off that the hairs are softened and you get a closer shave than if you shaved dry before taking a shower. TIP #2: I remembered my father telling me years ago when micro-screen shavers came out that you get the best shave by moving the shaver in a circular motion. I started out with this razor using the usual up/down motion, but when I noticed there were a few spots that didn't seem like they'd gotten a close shave, I went over them in a circular motion and got an extremely close shave. TIP #3: On initial use, be sure to charge the razor for at least 15 hours. I was eager to use mine and tried to used it 12 hours after iniating the charge, and thought the razor was defective because it didn't work. I tried it a couple of hours later, and it worked fine. TIP #4: Clean heads with little brush provided after each use as directed. It's easy to do by just flipping up the top and only takes 2 seconds, so if it will keep the razor in good working order, I would recommend following the directions. PROS: Has countertop charger so you don't have to plug a bulky base directy into the outlet and the base has a slot to keep the little head cleaner handy. Kudos to Remington for making this fabulous razor.
