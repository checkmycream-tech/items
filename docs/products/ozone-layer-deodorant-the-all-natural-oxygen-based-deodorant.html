So, depending on how sensitive you are to citronella, and if you like it at all, you may want to get the unscented version. I'll definitely go with the unscented version next time as I'm not too crazy about smelling like insect repellent. But then again, the smell might have the added benefit of keeping bugs away (wink, wink).<br /><br />Something else I find odd about this deodorant is there's no expiration date anywhere on the bottle. The bottle is also quite small for the price. I know on paper it's supposed to last for a while, but in real-life, everyday usage, I'm not sure how much bang for the buck we'll get.<br /><br />So, in conclusion, this deodorant really works as advertised and is effective in neutralizing odor, but it needs improving when it comes to the lavender scent and the packaging (no expiration date). For this reason, I'm only giving it 3 stars.
