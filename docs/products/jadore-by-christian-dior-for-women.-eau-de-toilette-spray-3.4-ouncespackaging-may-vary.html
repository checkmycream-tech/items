It is very different from Miss Dior or Miss Dior Cherie. The house of Dior has been producing perfume for a very long time and it's obvious they have an excellent perfumier. The floral notes in Jadore are very subtle yet it isn't a fragrance for those who are dubious about florals. It's definitely for those women who gravitate toward them and embrace them wholeheartedly. I personally love florals and have a large collection of them. And yet, this one is different from anything else I have.
